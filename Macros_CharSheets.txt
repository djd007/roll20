###############################################################################
All of these need to be customized for your character sheet.
Roll20 5e character sheets start counting everything at 0 so if you have 3
attacks on your sheet they will be numbered as 0, 1, 2.  So on your character
sheet copy the NPC_Attack#0, NPC_Attack#1, and make a new one for NPC_Attack#3
editing the paramater to be %selected|repeating_npcaction_$2_npc_action.

For the spells you will also need to customize it to how many spells you want
displayed per level. You can skip spells you don't use often or want displayed.

For the stats and traits you need to customize it to how many stats and traits
you want displayed.
###############################################################################
